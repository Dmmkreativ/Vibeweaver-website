Vibeweaver

VibeWeaver is a music app focused on creating a personalized soundscape based on your mood and activities.

AI-Powered
Soundscapes

VibeWeaver uses AI to dynamically mix and match music with ambient sounds (e.g., nature, cafe noise, white noise) to create a fully immersive soundscape.

Focus
Mode

VibeWeaver offers a focus mode that blocks notifications and creates a distraction-free listening environment for activities that require concentration.

Social
Sharing

Users can share their custom soundscapes with friends or on social media platforms.
